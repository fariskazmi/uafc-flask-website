Teachers

Sign up on our form and we will get in touch to better understand your needs and match you or your classroom with a pair of mentors. Bring Future Creators to your school and give students in grades 7-12 the chance to build their own technology projects.

1. After school program at your school

Future Creators comes to your school (now offering digital delivery!). We work with you to find what is best for your schedule, and establish a once-a-week after school program where a pair of mentors work with your students.

2. Choose a project

Our mentors help your students choose a project that inerests them. This can be anything from building drones to robot cars!

3. We help make it a reality

Our mentors provide the resources, knowledge, and guidance in weekly after school sessions to bring your students' projects to life.
