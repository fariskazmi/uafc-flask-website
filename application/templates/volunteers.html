Volunteers

Do you have a passion for engineering and technology?

Sign up on our form and we will reach out to chat with you. Even if you've never built a technology project before, no need to worry! We provide all the training and guidance you need. For more challenging projects, you will also have access to our University of Alberta Engineering Faculty partners for support. We can teach you cool stuff like this or even this. What do volunteers do?

Develop

Help us develop new project ideas, from drones to machine learning!

Mentor

You and a partner will be matched with students in grades 7-12 to provide after school mentorship and help them build thier own project.
