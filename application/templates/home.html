Future Creators

Powering the creators of tomorrow

Empowering youth to create their own technology projects

Future Creators is a University of Alberta Engineering student group that seeks to inspire future engineers and technology innovators by providing youth with the mentors, knowledge, and resources to bring their own technology projects to life. We partner with schools to provide a weekly afterschool program to students in grades 7-12
